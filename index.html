About InkStain

exploring edgy and soulful designs with a vintage heartbeat, InkStain is a concept-driven tattoo portfolio focused on dark art. This project explores bold compositions and symbolic storytelling through tattoo design. Built as both a creative and front-end development project, it reflects my journey into design, structure, and user experience.

Based in Malta — private studio sessions available by appointment only.
